:EIBQC: Expert Infantryman Badge Qualification Course

The Expert Infantryman Badge Qualification Course is a course in which your skills as an infantryman will be harshly tested. Those who complete this course will receive the Expert Infantryman Qualification, which proves their exceptional skills in combat and land navigation, among other things.

Requirements
  • Rank: E3+
  • Service: 2 weeks minimum
  • Device: Desktop only (no mobile)
  • Equipment: Working microphone
  • Security: 2FA on Roblox/Discord
  • Record: Clean 1 month
  • Unit: Infantry unit (USAR)

:Sharpshooter: Marksmanship Qualification Course

The Marksmanship Qualification Course has a 2 badge device which is the Expert badge and the Sharpshooter badge. It is given to those who successfully complete the Marksmanship Qualification Course with the required points. Those who complete the course will receive the Sharpshooter or the Expert badge.

Requirements
  • Rank: E3+
  • Service: 2 weeks minimum
  • Device: Desktop only (no mobile)
  • Unit: Infantry unit (USAR)
  • Security: 2FA on Roblox/Discord
  • Record: Clean 1 month
